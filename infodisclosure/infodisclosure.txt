Information Disclosure involves the unauthorized access or exposure of private or sensitive information. This can happen through various vectors such as software vulnerabilities, inadequate data protection measures, or human error, leading to the exposure of personal data, trade secrets, or any information that was meant to be confidential. Protecting against information disclosure requires robust data encryption, access controls, and privacy policies
